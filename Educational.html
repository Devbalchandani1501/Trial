Academic Amenities

The School Library

The spacious school library is stocked with periodicals, newspapers and an impressive index of titles, covering both fiction and non-fiction. These have been selected for the informative and educational value, catering to all age groups. Children are encouraged to make full use of these facilities in order to foster a healthy reading habit and satiate the inquisitive mind.The library is managed by highly qualified library personnel. In addition, the library also features an integrated audio-visual suite with internet facility to complement the research activities.

ICT labs

The School has 2 ICT labs and more than 5 trolleys which consist of 36 laptops/iPad for students. All computers are equipped with multimedia kits and interconnected nodes with internet facilities. Technology is used to make teaching and learning meaningful and fun. ICT is well integrated into all subject areas. New technology tools like Interactive LED boards have been installed in all classrooms and learning areas. Science Labs

To meet the requirements of our Senior Middle, Secondary and Senior Secondary students,state-of-the-art labs (for General Science, Math, Physics, Chemistry and Biology) are provided with excellent equipment and facilities , ensuring safe learning experience in a spacious and distinct setup, in concurrence with the CBSE norms. A STEAM lab is also designed to ensure student success through a combination of text, videos, ICT and real-world, hands-on activities. As against the traditional teacher-centric classroom model, children learn by way of enquiry, exploration, questioning, debate, application, collaboration, challenge
